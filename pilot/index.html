Passion
for
cycling

I sin nye hoppy finder Tirill ro og får et
break fra den travle by og den travle brhverdag.
Hun nyder det sociale
og motionen man får
i andledningen
af en cykeltur.

- I have been cycling for only a few monts,
so its a fairly new hobby of mine.
But for a few months I have experienced
that this is really my passion

- I love biking because
it makes you get out of
the city, where
it’s so much noise,
so big buildings.

- 3 words to describe
my biking?
